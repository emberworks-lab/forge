# ADR criteria

Only offer to create an ADR when all three conditions are true:

1. **Hard to reverse** — the cost of changing your mind later is meaningful.
2. **Surprising without context** — a future reader will wonder "why did they do it this way?"
3. **Result of a real trade-off** — there were genuine alternatives and you picked one for specific reasons.

If any of the three is missing, skip the ADR. Most decisions don't need one.
